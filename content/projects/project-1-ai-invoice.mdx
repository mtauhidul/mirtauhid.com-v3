## What is InvoNexus?

![InvoNexus Banner](/projects/invonexus/invonexus-2.png)

**InvoNexus** is an <span style={{ color: '#FF4500' }}>**AI-powered web application**</span> designed to help businesses and institutions <span style={{ color: '#32CD32' }}>**organize invoices efficiently**</span>. Quickly find any invoice with a single search or click, keep track indefinitely, and eliminate the hassle of physically managing invoices from vendors.

## How It Works

![Workflow](/projects/invonexus/invonexus-4.png)

1. **AI-Driven Invoice Processing**: Automatically tracks incoming invoice emails sent to your dedicated business email address.
2. **Invoice Extraction**: Once an invoice is received, the AI verifies and extracts data, turning unstructured content into structured, usable information.
3. **Verification & Formatting**: Data is organized into a <span style={{ color: '#00CED1' }}>**manageable format**</span> ensuring accurate, consistent records.
4. **Interactive Dashboard**: Effortlessly manage, track, and locate invoices through a comprehensive, user-friendly interface.

## Technologies Used

InvoNexus is a <span style={{ color: '#4682B4' }}>**full-stack application**</span> built with advanced technologies:

- **Frontend**: React and Material UI for intuitive, responsive design.
- **Backend**: Dual Node.js and Express servers for seamless mail processing.
- **Database**: MongoDB ensures scalable, efficient data management.
- **AI Invoice Processing**: Utilizes OpenAI Mindee for accurate data extraction.
- **Cloud Storage**: Secure invoice storage with Cloudinary.
- **Hosting**: Deployed on Heroku for reliable scalability.
